    Why is it important that You Stay Home


    • Our mind is a trigger of our words, what we say it imagines and starts working on it. Our surroundings affect the most of it. During this hard time, being positive must be our utmost concern.

    • People usally have the habit of acting as their own doctor and hence, it must be avoided. Self-medication equals to self-poisoning and therefore, consulting a doctor is a must.

    • Covid-19 vaccines are equally safe and effective and therefore, without having and 2nd thoughts one should get vaccinated at the earliest. However, it is advisable to consult a doctor in case of any uncertainty.

    • Maintaining hygiene and wearing masks are indispensable. It is advised to use alcohol based sanitizers and surgical masks, properly tucked and hence, covered by another layer of mask to prevent infection.

    Who we are

    Wake With Wellness is a community based organization registered under UNITED NATIONS to exercise good actions for the rehabilitation of communities regarding health and social awareness without ascertaining the foundations of legal norms. We’ll create health awareness campaigns that will include posters, content creation, interactive sessions and imparting information that can increase the awareness of people in the country about palliative and health care.

    There are six working departments -

    • Public Relations Department
    • Editorial Department
    • Marketing Department
    • Events Department
    • Design/Media Department

    World Environment Day 2021

    Our environment is the most valuable gift to humans, as well as to all living creatures, because it provides the resources needed to sustain life on Earth. The environment is made up of all living things, including water, air, soil, and vegetation. However, the contamination and pollution of these valuable resources has increased. Trees are cut down, soil erosion occurs, and water bodies and the air are polluted. As a result, it is important to raise awareness in order to preserve the ecosystem for current and future generations.
